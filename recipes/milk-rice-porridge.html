Milk rice porridge

Important! The recipe for a multicooker. As a result, it turns out a very tasty porridge, moderate in sweetness and salt.

Ingredients:

  • Rice 150 g (washed)
  • 6 tsp sugar
  • Less than half a tsp salt
  • 350 ml water
  • 300 ml milk
  • (Optional) EGGS

The cooking process:

  1. Wash Rice
  2. Put 6 tsp sugar, Less than half a tsp salt
  3. Fill in 350 ml water and 300 ml milk
  4. Put it in the multicooker for cooking on the appropriate program
  5. (Optional) Serve with boiled eggs
