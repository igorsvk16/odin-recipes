Milkshake

Important, you need a blender. The result is a delicious cocktail.

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 4 blueberries
  • 3 tablespoons of grated strawberries (with sugar)
  • 500 ml of milk
  • (Optional) You can add a portion of protein

The cooking process:

  1. Put all the ingredients in a blender container
  2. Stir with a blender for about 30 seconds.
